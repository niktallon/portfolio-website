About

Bio

Ive been playing music since I was 11 years old. Classical saxophone to be exact. My entire formal education was based around classical music study, composition, and ensemble playing. While in grad school, I started working in audio engineering and realized we needed a way to stream our concerts online, and get people to listen to them. That was the moment my music world collided with digital design and marketing strategy quite the harmonic convergence.

I learned web design, digital advertising and analytics, and steered myself toward mission-driven organizations in search of practical digital solutions. So far Ive overhauled the digital experience for organizations like Volunteers of America, the SF-Marin Food Bank, and SFFILM.

Music, and being part of an ensemble, is at the core of how I work. I create successful user-centered design projects through collaboration. I fine tune client goals, listen and perform focused research, and then educate the team on how design will help them reach, retain, and even delight their audiences.
